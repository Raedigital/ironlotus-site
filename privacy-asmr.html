ASMR Satisfy — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Overview

ASMR Satisfy is developed by Iron Lotus Personal Training Ltd. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

ASMR Satisfy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no network connectivity required.

Local Storage

The app does not store any data on your device. There are no accounts, no saved preferences, and no usage tracking.

Analytics

ASMR Satisfy does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services.

Health Data

While ASMR Satisfy can be used as a supplementary tool for finger sensitivity exercises, it does not collect, store, or share any health-related data. It is not a medical device and does not access Apple HealthKit or any health frameworks.

Children's Privacy

ASMR Satisfy does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
