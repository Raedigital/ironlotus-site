BreathMax — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Overview

BreathMax is developed by Iron Lotus Personal Training Ltd. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

BreathMax does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Microphone Usage

BreathMax uses the device microphone solely to detect breathing patterns during the breathing exercise. Audio is processed in real-time on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. No audio data leaves your device at any time.

Screen Time / App Blocking

BreathMax may use Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls) to temporarily shield selected apps until a breathing exercise is completed. The list of apps you choose to shield is stored locally on your device and is never shared with us or any third party. BreathMax does not monitor, log, or transmit your app usage data.

Local Storage

The app may store your preferences (such as which apps to shield) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics

BreathMax does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services.

Health Data

While BreathMax can be used as a supplementary tool for breathing exercises and finger sensitivity rehabilitation, it does not collect, store, or share any health-related data. It is not a medical device and does not access Apple HealthKit or any health frameworks.

Children's Privacy

BreathMax does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
