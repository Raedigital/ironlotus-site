Kook Dodger - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Kook Dodger is developed by Iron Lotus Personal Training Ltd. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Kook Dodger does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • High scores
  • Custom character avatar images you upload
  • Daily challenge progress

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics

Kook Dodger does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Kook Dodger does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
